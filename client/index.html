Supports Twitter bookmark HTML/JSON or Instagram saved_posts.json

Where to get export archives:

  • X / Twitter: Go to Settings > Your Account > Download an archive of your data. The export contains a file named `bookmarks.js` or `bookmarks.json` in your data folder.
  • Instagram: Go to Account Center > Your Information and Permissions > Download your information. Request standard JSON format, select "Saved Items" only. Look for `saved_posts.json` inside the ZIP.
